# El Tianguis — Official Art Style Guide

**Style Name:** Casual Market Cartoon  
**Status:** LOCKED — Single source of truth for all visual assets.

---

## 1. Style Goal
A casual cartoon style inspired by marketplace tycoon games such as *Dealer's Life*, with a slightly more modern and expressive look. The style must feel lively, colorful, readable, and consistent with a Mexican street market (tianguis) environment.

---

## 2. General Art Rules
*   **Proportions:** Cartoon semi-realistic proportions.
*   **Line Art:** Clean line art.
*   **Shading:** Soft shading (1–2 layers maximum).
*   **Detail Level:** Medium detail level.
*   **Characters:** Expressive characters.
*   **Colors:** Warm saturated colors.
*   **Readability:** Optimized for mobile game readability.

### ❌ Do NOT Generate:
*   Photorealistic art
*   Painterly illustrations
*   Heavy gradients
*   Hyper-detailed textures
*   Semi-realistic portrait illustration styles
*   Mixed art styles

---

## 3. Color Style
*   **Primary Palette Inspiration:** Mexican street markets (tianguis).
*   **Rules:** Colors should be warm, saturated, and readable.
*   **Backgrounds:** Must be desaturated and darker than interactive elements.

---

## 4. Visual Hierarchy
1.  **NPC Character** (Primary Focus)
2.  **Item being traded**
3.  **Negotiation buttons**
4.  **Background** (Lowest Priority)

*The background must never compete visually with the NPC or UI.*

---

## 5. NPC Design Rules
*   **Height:** Approximately 1.2–1.4× the stall height.
*   **Proportions:** Cartoon semi-realistic.
*   **Detail Level:** Medium.
*   **Expressions Required:** 4 variants (Neutral, Thinking, Happy, Annoyed).
*   **Clothing Style:** Casual modern clothing suitable for street markets.
*   **❌ Avoid:** Photorealistic faces, hyper-realistic anatomy, complex skin shading.

---

## 6. Item Design Rules
*   **Style:** Simple, recognizable, slightly stylized.
*   **Readability:** Must be readable at small sizes.
*   **Icon Size Guideline:** 256–512 px.

---

## 7. Environment Design Rules
*   **Aesthetic:** Stalls and environment must look handmade and colorful.
*   **Elements:** Tarps, crates, hanging lights, simple market props.
*   **❌ Avoid:** Overly detailed patterns.

---

## 8. UI Style Rules
*   **Aesthetic:** Rounded, colorful, high contrast.
*   **Readability:** Easy to read on mobile.
*   **❌ Avoid:** Minimalistic flat UI.

---

## 9. Scale Rules
*   **NPC:** ≈ 1.3× stall height.
*   **Item Card:** ≈ 25–30% screen width.
*   **UI:** Anchored to bottom area.

---

## 10. Asset Size Guidelines
*   **NPC Sprite Size:** 512×1024 or 768×1024 px.
*   **Item Icons:** 256×256 to 512×512 px.
*   **Constraint:** All sprites must be trimmed and centered.

---

## 11. Consistency Rule
All generated visual assets must follow the **Casual Market Cartoon style**. If a generated asset does not match the style, it must be rejected and regenerated.
